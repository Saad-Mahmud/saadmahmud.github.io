I am a 2nd-year MS/PhD student under Shlomo Zilberstein at UMass Amherst. My current research focuses on analyzing and debugging autonomous systems for safe deployment. Before starting at UMass, I was a full-time Research Assistant at Cognitive Agents and Interaction Lab (CAIL), University of Dhaka, where I worked on Multi-Agent Systems. During this time, I also worked as a Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology for a while.
