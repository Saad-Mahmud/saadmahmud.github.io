I am a first-year Ph.D. student at UMass Amherst. My current research work revolves around the area of Human-centered AI and its application to Autonomous Vehicles. Before starting my Ph.D. at UMass, I was a full-time Research Assistant at Cognitive Agents and Interaction Lab (CAIL), University of Dhaka, where I work on Multi-Agent Systems. During this time, I also worked as a Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology for a while.
