I am a fourth-year PhD student at UMass Amherst, working under the guidance of Professor Shlomo Zilberstein. My research focuses on developing methods for effective communication among decision-making agents, with applications in Planning, Reinforcement Learning, and Multi-Agent Systems. Currently, I am exploring the use of LLM agents for understanding human intent and generating explainable plans. My broader interests include enhancing collaboration between AI and humans in complex decision-making environments. You can find my complete CV here.
