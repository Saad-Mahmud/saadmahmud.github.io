I am a full-time research assistant at Cognitive Agents and Interaction Lab (CAIL), University of Dhaka. Before joining the lab, I was an undergrad at the CSE department, University Of Dhaka. My current research interest includes:
  • Multi-agent Systems (Coordination and Planning, Multi-agent RL, Game Theory).
  • Interaction Between Human and Robot/AI.
  • AI for Social Impact and Computational Sustainability (Applying Game-theory and RL for social good).
  • Decentralized Optimization and Inference (Constraint Network, Factor Graphs, PGMs)
Although these are well established and separate subfields of AI, a common recurring theme in all of these is the interaction among multiple intelligent entities (Robots, Human, Society as a whole). The interesting patterns and complex challenges that emerge from these interactions that ignites my curiosity. You can download my complete CV here: [CV]
